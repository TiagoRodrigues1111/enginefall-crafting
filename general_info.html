Registering into a new class:

You can register into class 3,2,1 and conductor at carriages: C08, C05, C02 and C00, respectively. After registering into a new class you get some rewards:

Barrier Breaking Rewards:

Breaking the Barriers in the undercarriages between class 3 and 2 and between class 2 and 1 is a great way of progressing through the train. The first barrier has a health of 10000, whereas the second barrier has a health of 15000. Breaking either will give you 3 common isotopes.

Base Building:

You can build a base on almost all carriages of the train. To do so, you will require a Build tool. With that build tool, you can build, upgrade, repair, and disassemble. In the build section, you can make:

  • Foundation
  • Wall
  • Door
  • Window

Karma:

You recieve karma everytime you destroy a Trash Bag, or donate to the train. For now, karma is useless, with only a cosmetic change to the name of the player.

Action Karma Reward
Destroy Trash Bag +0.1
Destroy Furniture -0.05
etc... 0

Trash Bags:

Trash Bags can be found in all of the carriages (except C00), and are one of the few things that give positive karma. There are 4 items that can be found by destroying them:

  • Rust Dew
  • Mushroom
  • Cola
  • Roach Nest

As far as I'm aware, the probability of getting any of them is the same, although the current sample size used to reverse engineer the stats is low.

Recycler Rewards:

In the Recycler, you can get back some of the resources used to create most items. Furthermore, you can also trade 5 parts for frames and microprocessors:

  • Pistol Frame
  • SMG Frame
  • Rifle Frame
  • Shotgun Frame
  • Microprocessor
  • Industrial Frame

As far as I'm aware, the probability of getting any of them is the same, although the current sample size used to reverse engineer the stats is low.

Farming Station:

There are two resources that can be used in the Farming Station: Mushroom, and Roach Nest. It takes 10s to produce 1 item. You can produce an infinite amount of both Mushrooms and Roach Nests with them.
