Enginefall Crafting Tool

This is the home page for the Enginefall Playtest Crafting Tool. This tool is not made by the official development team, it's a project from a fan of the game and the work they are doing. Furthermore, some of the information might became outdated with the progress of the game. For example, the prices of blueprints have changed in the past, and they might change again. Feel free to notify me on any potencial changes on the currently provided information. Any help is welcome!

Website Contents:

In this website, you will find:

  • A crafting tool for recipies, along with their curresponding information;
  • A tool for checking the prices of items in the shop;
  • Some general information about the game.
